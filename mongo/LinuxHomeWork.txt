В контейнере с ubuntu, в директории tmp, создать файл homework2.txt 
в которое поместить несколько строк текста. 
Потом вывести содержимое файла в консоль.

docker exec -it bubuntu bash
cd tmp

touch homework2.txt

printf "She walks in beauty, like the night\n
Of cloudless climes and starry skies;\n
And all that's best of dark and bright\n
Meet in her aspect and her eyes:\n
Thus mellowed to that tender light\n
Which heaven to gaudy day denies.\n" >> homework2.txt

cat homework2.txt
